Beyond Kind

WHO WE ARE AND WHAT DO WE DO?

Beyond Kind serves as a platform between wishful donors and NGOs across the country. NGOs plays a major role in the development of nation by supplementing the efforts of the Government. Beyond Kind brings you opportunities to help people in need by connecting you to numerous NGOs listed on this website. Our objective is to make the act of donation easier so that it encourages the community to make donations in a short amount of time without putting in so much effort. We give you the freedom to help the needy by donating generously or conducting any social event for people’s welfare.

WE CATER EVERY SECTION

NGOs are widespread accross this country dealing with various day to day activities and problems. Find NGOs focusing on different sectors in the society such as - Education , Disaster Relief , Common Welfare , Environment and many more.
